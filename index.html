What is Origami?

Origami is the art of paper folding, which is often associated with Japanese culture. In modern usage, the word "origami" is used as an inclusive term for all folding practices, regardless of their culture of origin.

Interesting Facts about this innocent creature- Pigeon

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  3. Pigeons are highly sociable animals
  4. Pigeons mate for life, and tend to raise two chicks at the same time.
  5. Both female and male pigeons share responsibility of caring for and raising young.

Funny and Wonderful Facts about Chamelions-"The natural chamoflaguers"

  1. Their feet work like salad tongs.
  2. Almost half of all known species live in Madagascar
  3. Chamelions vary wildly in terms of size
  4. They mainly change colour in order to communicate or regulate body temperature
  5. Skin Crystals enable them to change colour at will
  6. Unlike most of the lizards the tails of the chamelions cannot be regrown.

AmazingFacts about the "Black and White"-Panda

  1. They have great camouflage for their environment.
  2. Their eyes are different to normal bears.
  3. Cubs are well protected in their first month and they are courageous little animals.
  4. They serve as an helping hand
  5. They spend a lot of their day eating. But they do occasionally eat something other than bamboo and Bamboo is critical to their diet.

Astonishing Facts about "The Ship of the Desert"-Camels

  1. There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  2. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  3. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.
